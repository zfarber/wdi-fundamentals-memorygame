Intructions

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-sujaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cards are laid face down on a surface and two cards are flipped up over each turn. The object of the game is two turn over pairs of matching cards
(Queen-Queen or King-King).
